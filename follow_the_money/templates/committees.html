Committee Donations

Committee's Donations Given
About the Data

Data Source: Political Campaign Donations for the 2022 Campaign Cycle. Link: FEC Government 2022 Donation Data Download

Data dictionaries are included at the link above.

Committees are donators to politicians, and they can be organizations, companies, or political action groups. Committees have organization types that they classify themselves as. Organization types include cooperatives, corporations, corporations without capital stock, labor organizations, membership organizations, or trade organizations. However, most committees are unclassified. Committee donations show up as Transactions for candidates.

All Committees with Detail

This chart lets users see which committees were the most donated to, and select the committee from the left to see a detailed breakdown of the candidates it donated to, ordered by the donatation amount. This chart lists committees in the left column ordered by their total donation amounts, filters and legends for the results in the middle column, and detailed view of candidates for a selected committee on the right column.

Users can select one or more committees (using "Ctrl hold + click") from the left chart and see their candidate breakdown on the right.

  • Users can filter the left chart by specifying specific committees by typing their name and selecting the matching committee in the dropdown menu.
  • Users can filter to include donations to candidates only from selected states, from selected partys, or from selected office types. This will change the size of bars on the left and the rows on the detailed chart on the right.
  • Users can filter to include committees of specified organization types.
  • Users can specify a range for total donation amounts to display on the left chart. User can also filter the detailed chart on the right to include donations to candidates within a specified range.
  • Users may hover over names and marks on the chart for tooltips that include greater detail. If a tooltip has an "*" value, that means there are multiple values for that field. The tooltip also has an option to "View Data" if the hover is held.

    • Committee's Candidate Breakdown and Comparisons

    Users must type in, search, and select 2 committees from the drop down menu to compare the candidates they donated to. Committees will show up on the columns, candidates are on the rows. The length of the bars are the total transaction amount that a candidate received from the committee in that column. For a given candidate, if both committees have a non-zero transaction amount on that row then they both donated to that politician.

    Committee's Map for Candidate Donations

    User must select a committee and the map will display marks for the candidates it donated to.

  • The map can be zoomed in and out at the cursor using the mouse wheel.
  • The map can be panned by holding "Shift" and dragging the cursor.
  • Double clicking the map will zoom in at the cursor.
  • Hovering your cursor on the map will let a vertical navigation panel appear on the left. The magnifying glass lets the user search for a specific place on the map and zooms the view accordingly. The home button (house icon) will reset the view of the map.

  • Users may adjust the range of the Total Donation Amount to display on the map or filter out some candidates by their elected office type or party.
